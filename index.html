Vision

A society that ensures the welfare of women returnees and families, where everyone is free to exercise his/her right to work, have meaningful life, equal opportunities and access to social services.

Mission

Expand its membership, to Empower Women, to Advocate Migrant Women's Rights, locally and Internationally.

Goals

Shall establish a consolidated organization where members are united to implement its mission.

Shall contribute to the economic security for its members.

Shall establish a strong partnership and linkage with local and international women's organizations, similarly tasks org., church and academic institutions, humanitarian organizations, individual and private institutions.

Batis Center for Women is a non-profit orgaization that works to assist distressed Filipino women migrants and their families, under the roof of Batis Center is Batis AWARE (Association of Women in Action for Rights and Empowerment) an independent peoples organization with its vision to empower the returned migrants from all over the world. Batis Aware seeks to respond to the specific needs of the distressed women migrant workers, their children, and their families through various programs while informing and educating the general public on their plight to generate a strong public opinion against their exploitation and continued deployment. Critical to our empowerment program are capacity building initiatives, particularly opportunities for employment, livelihood, and growth so that they are not forced to work overseas due to poverty.
